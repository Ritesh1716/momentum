How It Works

in 4 steps

1
Sign in with Google
One tap to create your account. No email, no password, no friction. Your data syncs to cloud instantly and stays private.
2

Choose from 12 ready-made templates or create your own. Set daily goals, reminders, and categories. Takes under 60 seconds.

Works Everywhere

Install it like an app

Momentum is a PWA — no App Store needed. Install directly from Chrome for a native app experience.

🤖
Android (Best Experience)
1
Open Chrome and visit app.momentum-trackerapp.com
2
Tap the ⋮ menu (top right)
3
Tap "Add to Home Screen"
4
Opens like a native app with background notifications!
🍎
iPhone / iPad
1
Open Safari (must be Safari, not Chrome)
2
Tap the Share button at the bottom
3
Tap "Add to Home Screen"
4
Works great — note: iOS has limited background notifications
💻
Desktop / Laptop
1
Open Chrome on your computer
2
Click the install icon in address bar (or ⋮ menu)
3
Click "Install" — opens as standalone window
4
Same data as your phone — always synced!
